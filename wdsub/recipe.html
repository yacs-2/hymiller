Grandma's Brownie Recipe

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat the oven to 350 F
    • Rest the butter to room temperature
    • Wash your hands

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients

    1. In a large bowl, add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, and 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of coco powder. Stir indredents thoroughly.
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 tablespoon of baking soda
    3. Set aside.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, break 2 large eggs. Whisk until consistency
    2. Add 1 tbsp of vinilla extract and half a tsp of salt to the eggs.
    3. Pour half a coup of milk to the mixture

  4. Combine Mixtures

    1. Place the large mixing bowl with the dry ingredents in a stand mixer on the low setting
    2. Slowly pour the wet ingredednts until incorporated.
    3. Slowly pour 8 tbsp of the room temp. butter into the mixer

  5. Pour and Bake

    1. Grease a 8 by 11 in pan.
    2. Pour mix into the pan, and smooth it out.
    3. Place in oven to bake for 20min or until desired consistency

  6. Enjoy
